About Me

Hello! My name is Tim Albrecht. I am currently enrolled in the UNC Coding Bootcamp.

I have been a resident of Chapel Hill for the last 12 years. I started as an undergraduate at UNC, where I received a BS in Environmental Sciences. I then moved on to be a staff environmental scientist with RTI International for the next four years. A new opportunity arose, and I chose to leave RTI to start my own Fiber Optic Company, which I actively operated for the past three years. However, I came to miss my previous passions, as well as the limited home time, so opted to return to the environmental sector.

As I continue to work this coding program, I hope to begin applying my gained knowledge toward environmentally-themed applications. With passions in energy and city sustainability, I hope to find a unique opportunity to bridge my past and present.
